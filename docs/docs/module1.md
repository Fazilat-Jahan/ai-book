# Module 1: Foundations of AI and Robotics

This module introduces the fundamental concepts of Artificial Intelligence and robotics, laying the groundwork for the more advanced topics to come.

## Key Topics

-   **Introduction to Artificial Intelligence:** History, definitions, and key milestones.
-   **Types of AI:** Narrow, General, and Super AI.
-   **Introduction to Robotics:** History, components of a robot, and types of robots.
-   **The Intersection of AI and Robotics:** How AI is transforming the field of robotics.
-   **Mathematical Foundations:** A refresher on linear algebra and calculus for AI.
-   **Introduction to Python for AI:** Essential libraries like NumPy, and Pandas.
